HORATIAN

horatian>IN +stanzas+ STANZAS, EACH OF +h_lines+ LINES, DESCRIBE +hero+, +gradient+.|IN +stanzas+ STANZAS, EACH OF +h_lines+ LINES, DESCRIBE +hero+, +gradient+. +extra+.
gradient>PROGRESSING GENTLY FROM+ +concept_statement
concept_statement>concept+ TO +concept2
concept>THE +cognizing+ OF THE +abstraction+ OF +object|THE +cognizing2+ OF THE +abstraction2+ OF +object
concept2>THE +cognizing2+ OF THE +abstraction+ OF +object|THE +cognizing+ OF THE +abstraction2+ OF +object
abstraction>PAIN|SADNESS|LOSS|HUMOR|FRAILTY|YEARNING
abstraction2>LOSS|GROWTH|MERCY|HISTORY|TIMELESSNESS|MORTALITY
cognizing>AWARENESS|FORGIVENESS|HATRED|NON-AWARENESS|DISTANT APPRECIATION|MISUNDERSTANDING
cognizing2>ACCEPTANCE|PERCEPTION|REGRETTING|UNDERSTANDING
object>hero|hero|hero|hero|LIFE IN GENERAL|villain
stanzas>2|4|6
h_lines>2|4
extra>REMARK IN THE +count_line+ LINE UPON +extra_object
extra_object>THE +h_feature+ OF +hero|WINE|WINE|A MORSEL

count_line>1ST|rel_num|rel_num+ TO LAST

PINDARIC

pindaric>᛫ +strophe+ ᛬ +antistrophe+ ⁖ +epode
strophe>IN A STANZA OF +p_lines+ +short_or_long+ +meter1+ LINES, ADOPTING A +style1+ STYLE, DESCRIBE +hero+, WITH PARTICULAR ATTENTION TO THE +p_feature+ OF +hero+.
meter1>IAMBIC|DACTYLLIC|DACTYLLIC|AEOLIC|LOGAOEDIC
meter2>meter_adjust+ +meter1|PYRRHIC
meter_adjust>NOT|ANYTHING BUT|ONLY IMPERFECTLY
p_lines>6|8|8|10|12
short_or_long>BRIEF|BRIEF|BRIEF|LENGTHY|LENGTHY|LENGTHY|ABSURDLY LONG
p_feature>LAUREL|BLADE|RUNE|RIVER|LAW|DECREE|GOVERNMENT|CRIME
style1>FEARSOME|SOLEMN|MYSTICAL|GNOMIC|JAGGED|SUBLIME|GOVERNMENTAL|MUSCULAR|LAUDATORY
style2>COMEDIC|EUPHORIC|REVERENT|BLOOD-THIRSTY|STERN|RECURSIVE|VITUPERATIVE|MENTIONING +p_trait
p_trait>THE STATE|A GOD|A WAR|A SPORT|A MORAL|A VIRTUE|A CONQUEST|A VICTORY|A NATURAL DISASTER
antistrophe>USING THE SAME STRUCTURE BUT NOW +style2+, CONTRAPOSE +hero+ WITH ITS INVERSE AND ENEMY, +villain+.
epode>NOW, WITH +subtract+ FEWER LINES, AND THESE +ep_style+, IMAGINE HOW +hero+ SHALL +do_to_villain+ +villain+.
